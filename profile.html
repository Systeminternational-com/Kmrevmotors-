Company Profile

Who We Are

KMR EV Motors is a premier authorized dealership for ELESCO Electric Vehicles, strategically located in Hyderabad. We stand at the forefront of the electric mobility revolution in India, committed to providing top-tier, technologically advanced two-wheelers to our community.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the leading catalyst in Hyderabad's transition to sustainable, zero-emission transportation by making premium electric vehicles accessible to everyone.

Mission

To deliver unparalleled customer service, transparent financial options (like our easy EMI facilities), and reliable after-sales support to ensure absolute customer satisfaction.
