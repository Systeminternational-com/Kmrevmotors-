The Economic and Environmental Benefits of Switching to ELESCO EVs

Published on: October 2024

The shift towards electric vehicles (EVs) is no longer a trend; it is a necessity. At KMR EV Motors, we see daily how making the switch to an ELESCO electric scooter transforms our customers' lives.

Economic Savings: With traditional petrol prices constantly fluctuating, fuel costs can take a significant toll on a household budget. ELESCO scooters offer an incredible cost-per-kilometer ratio. Charging a Lithium-Ion battery for a full 180 KM range costs a fraction of the equivalent petrol fill-up. When you factor in our easy EMI options, the scooter essentially pays for itself in fuel savings over time.

Environmental Impact: Transportation is a major contributor to urban air pollution. By switching to a zero-emission ELESCO vehicle, you are actively participating in reducing carbon footprints.

How KMR EV Motors is Driving the Electric Revolution in Hyderabad

Published on: September 2024
